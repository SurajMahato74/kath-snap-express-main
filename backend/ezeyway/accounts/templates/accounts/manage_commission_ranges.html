How Commission System Works
Commission Rules
  • Commission is deducted only when order status is "delivered"
  • Commission is deducted from vendor's wallet balance
  • If wallet balance is insufficient, order cannot be marked as delivered
  • Ranges are checked from lowest to highest amount
Example

Order Amount: ₹750

Range: ₹500 - ₹1000 → Commission: ₹25

✓ ₹25 will be deducted from vendor wallet
